MyLittleJuniper

All products are handmade by me. I have a background in Costume Design and Construction, but started making goods for children when my sister become pregrant with her first. The baby blankets and burb clothes were a personal favorite of her's. Since then I have started designing and producing many other goods for children.


As of right now, the only products that come in bundles are the baby blankets and burb clothes. Please note, when purchasing a bundle patterns are picked at random and you may not get a matching set.
